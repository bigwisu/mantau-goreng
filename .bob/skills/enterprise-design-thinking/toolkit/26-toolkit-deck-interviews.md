INTRO DUCTI O N

Why do we do this activity?

If you want to get to know the people you serve,
there’s no substitute for going out and talking to
them. A good interview reveals the world as
others see it. This will help you build empathy
and identify new questions to ask.

When should you use this activity?

Pursue interviews anytime you want to validate a
hypothesis about your user, or better understand
how your ideas might impact them.

Write a script or
discussion guide to use
during the interview.

• Include general questions
about the interviewees’ job

60 minInterviewsCAP TU R E

As you conduct your
interview, record what
you hear without
judgement.

• If two interviewers are
present, make sure one
focuses primarily on capturing
key ﬁndings, and one focuses
primarily on asking questions.

reflections with anyone
involved in the interview.
