# IBM Enterprise Design Thinking Toolkit

Source: https://www.ibm.com/training/enterprise-design-thinking/toolkit

Guided activities to align your team and apply design thinking in your work today.

> **See also:** [IBM Enterprise Design Thinking Framework](00_framework.md) - The principles, loop, and keys that guide these activities

## Downloadable PDF Templates (in order of appearance)

### Define your AI strategy
2. [AI Essentials: Intent](02-toolkit-deck-ai-essentials-intent.md) - Determine why you would or wouldn't use AI to solve your users' problems
3. [AI Essentials: Data](03-toolkit-deck-ai-essentials-data.md) - Identify the data that you have and what data you need to get
4. [AI Essentials: Understanding](04-toolkit-deck-ai-essentials-understanding.md) - Deconstruct your data sources into the components that you need to teach your AI
5. [AI Essentials: Reasoning](05-toolkit-deck-ai-essentials-reasoning.md) - Revisit your big ideas and bring them down to earth
6. [AI Essentials: Knowledge](06-toolkit-deck-ai-essentials-knowledge.md) - Brainstorm the impact that your AI can have on people and the world

### Synthesize research
7. [As-is Scenario Map](07-toolkit-deck-as-is.md) - Build a better understanding of your users' current experience
8. [Empathy Map](08-toolkit-deck-empathy-map.md) - Build empathy for your users through a conversation informed by your team's observations
9. [Needs Statements](09-toolkit-deck-needs-statements.md) - Outline what users need in order to achieve their goals
10. [Stakeholder Map](10-toolkit-deck-stakeholder-map.md) - Identify project stakeholders, their expectations and their relationship to each other

### Plan your work
11. [Assumptions and Questions](11-toolkit-deck-assumptions-and-questions.md) - When your team needs a "reality check," identify your assumptions and the best ways to address them
12. [Experience-based Roadmap](12-toolkit-deck-experience-based-roadmap.md) - Break down your long-term experience into the most essential near-term outcomes for your user
13. [Hills](13-toolkit-deck-hills.md) - Use Hills to clearly state your intent in terms of user and market value
14. [Prioritization Grid](14-toolkit-deck-prioritization-grid.md) - Decide your next move by focusing on the intersection of importance and feasibility
15. [Research Plan](15-toolkit-deck-research-plan.md) - Define your goals and action steps to understand your users and address your team's unknowns
16. [User Stories](16-toolkit-deck-user-stories.md) - Describe requirements as discrete tasks a user must do to reach their goal

### Generate new ideas
17. [Big Idea Vignettes](17-toolkit-deck-big-idea-vignettes.md) - Rapidly diverge on a breadth of possible solutions to meet your users' needs
18. [Speculative Design Stories](18-speculative-design-stories.md) - Create short stories that predict your users' future problems and produce novel solutions to address those problems
19. [Storyboards](19-toolkit-deck-storyboards.md) - Communicate ideas through visual stories that showcase how they fit into your users' lives
20. [To-be Scenario Map](20-toolkit-deck-to-be.md) - Draft a vision of your user's future experience to show how your ideas address their current needs

### Test your ideas
21. [Cognitive Walkthrough](21-toolkit-deck-cognitive-walkthrough.md) - Observe users interacting with a prototype in order to evaluate its success
22. [Feedback Grid](22-toolkit-deck-feedback-grid.md) - Gather and organize feedback from users, team members or stakeholders
23. [Paper Prototypes](23-toolkit-deck-paper-prototypes.md) - Communicate your ideas quickly by using cheap and versatile materials
24. [Wireframes](24-toolkit-deck-wireframes.md) - Prototype digital user interfaces at low and medium fidelity to quickly get feedback

### Gather user data
25. [Contextual Inquiry](25-toolkit-deck-contextual-inquiry.md) - Observe users, in their daily context, to understand the "how" and "why" behind their behavior
26. [Interviews](26-toolkit-deck-interviews.md) - Get to know users or stakeholders by reaching out and talking to them

### Align your team
27. [Daily Syncs](27-toolkit-deck-daily-syncs.md) - Stay informed on the team's actions, ask for assistance, and surface blockers early
28. [Hopes and Fears](28-toolkit-deck-hopes-and-fears.md) - Learn and discuss each others' hopes and fears before starting a project or onboarding new team members
29. [Playbacks](29-toolkit-deck-playbacks.md) - Tell stories to share your work and exchange feedback
30. [Retrospectives](30-toolkit-deck-retrospectives.md) - Reflect together at the end of an iteration and find ways to improve the way you work together
